                            GENERAL AFFIDAVIT.

State of  South Carolina , County of  Beaufort                          , SS:
    In the matter of  Flora Robinson Widow of Harry
Robinson Co. G. 34th Reg't U.S.C.T.

    Personally came before me, a  Pension Notary      in and for aforesaid County
and State,  South Carolina
Sucius Robinson  Venus Ostone
and  Annie Barnwell  Amy Barnwell        , aged               years
citizen of the town of  Green Pond  , County of  Colleton              , State of
_____________________ , well known  to me to be reputable and entitled to credit, and who,

being duly sworn, declare in relation to aforesaid case as follows:

[NOTE.—Affiants should state how they gain a knowledge of the facts to which they testify.]

We know Flora Robinson Widow of Harry
Robinson. Co. G. 34th U.S.C.T.

We live on the same plantation with her.
She is old and in want. She owns
15 acres of poor land and old house.
She works a little ground and raises
a little crop.
         When she gets short of
provisions we help her out by
giving her a little provisions.

She is dependent upon us in a
great measure for support. The above
testimony was written in my presence
at Green Pond South Carolina by Jasmo. Crofoot
Pension notary from oral statements made
date below mentioned, not aided or prompted
by any written or printed statement prepared
or dictated by any other person.

We further declare that  we have  no interest in said case, and  are not con-
cerned in its prosecution.
