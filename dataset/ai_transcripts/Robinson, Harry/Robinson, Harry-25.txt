                            GENERAL AFFIDAVIT.

State of  South Carolina , County of  Beaufort                          , SS:
    In the matter of  Flora Robinson Widow of
Harry Robinson Co G. 34th Reg't U.S.C. Infy Vols

    Personally came before me, a  Pension Notary      in and for aforesaid County
and State,  William Washington          , aged  40      years
citizen of the town of  Green Pond  , County of  Colleton              , State of
South Carolina            , well known  to me to be reputable and entitled to credit, and who,

being duly sworn, declare in relation to aforesaid case as follows:

[NOTE.—Affiants should state how they gain a knowledge of the facts to which they testify.]

I know Flora Robinson Widow of
Harry Robinson. I have known her
for some years. I live and reside
near the Goff Plantation. and am a neighbor
to Flora Robinson. I know that Flora owns
some 15 acres of Pine Barren land on which
she lives. The land & house being worth
about 50 dollars. The old lady gets her
living on the place with the help of the
neighbors about her. She is quite old and
dependent. The above testimony was written in my
presence, at Green Pond South Carolina
By Jasper Crofoot Pension notary from oral statements made
date below mentioned, not aided or prompted by any written
or printed statement prepared or dictated by any other person.
         further declare that  I have  no interest in said case, and  am not con-
cerned in its prosecution.

1. _______________                      William Washington
                                        Signature of Affiant.
2. _______________

NOTE.—In the execution of papers and evidence, whenever a person or witness signs by mark, (†,) two persons who
can write must attest the signature by signing their names opposite.
The official before whom papers are executed is not a competent witness to a mark.
