ARMY OF THE UNITED STATES.
                    CERTIFICATE
        OF DISABILITY                          FOR DISCHARGE.


Private Harry Robinson, of Captain John Geo.
Hamel Company, (G) of the Thirty fourth Regiment of the United States
C. Troops was enlisted by Col. Jas. L. Montgomery of
the 34th Regiment of U.S.C.T. at Beaufort S.C.
on the first day of June, 1863, to serve three years; he was born
in Colubahee in the State of South Carolina, is Thirty first
years of age, six feet ______ inches high, black complexion, black eyes,
black hair, and by occupation when enlisted a Laborer. During the last two
months said soldier has been unfit for duty 61 days.* Said Private Harry Robinson
is at least fifty seven years of Age — has not done
duty since his enlistment in June 1863 on account
of old age and general physical disability.

STATION: Jacksonville Fla
DATE: May 16th 1865                     John Geo. Hamel
                                             Captain
                                        Commanding Company. G

    I CERTIFY, that I have carefully examined the said Private Harry Robinson
of Captain John G. Hamel Company, and find him incapable of performing the duties of a
soldier because of old age and general debility — He is
utterly worthless as a soldier and has so been for
the whole period of his long service. He is unfit
for the "Invalid Corps" on account of either.


                                        C and D Hansom,
                                             3d M Sct                 Surgeon.


DISCHARGED, this second day of June        , 186 5, at ______________
Jacksonville Fla.

                                        M. Geo. Hattery
                                        MCo 34 N.A.C.T.
                                                    Commanding the Reg't.
