                            GENERAL AFFIDAVIT.

State of South Carolina, County of Beaufort  Colleton                   SS:
    In the matter of  Flora Robinson Widow of Harry Robinson
of Co G. 34 Regiment USCT

    Personally came before me, a  Pension Notary      in and for aforesaid County
and State,  Flora Robinson           , aged about 73  years
and ___________________________    , aged _____________ years
citizen of the town of  Green Pond , County of  Colleton             , State of
South Carolina          , well known  to me to be reputable and entitled to credit, and who,
being duly sworn, declare  in relation to aforesaid case, as follows:

[NOTE.—Affiants should state how they gain a knowledge of the facts to which they testify.]

I reside on the Goff Plantation one mile
from the Greenbond R.Road Station, in Colleton Co
S.C.   I own 15 acres of fine Barren Lands
valued at, for which my husband agreed
to pay in his life time $3.00 per acre and since
his death I have managed by hard labor
to pay for. My home is a one story 3 Room
Clapboard House valued at not over $1000.
I plant a little crop about 2 acres in Cotton
Corn Peas and Potatoes. At times I get short of
provisions and ask my fellow Servants and
Neighbors to help me out and they give me
Potatoes and other food to assist me along.
This is the way I have managed to get along
since my husband's death in January 1874.
I have been sick for many years. I am
suffering and have but a short time to stay
in trouble on this Earth.
