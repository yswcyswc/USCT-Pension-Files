                            GENERAL AFFIDAVIT.

State of  South Carolina , County of  Colleton                          SS:
    In the matter of  Harry Robinson late of Co G. 34th Regiment
U.S.C.I.
On this  26      day of  May          A. D. one thousand eight hundred and
ninety- two, personally appeared before me  C. G. Henderson a Master
in Equity                    within and for the County and State
aforesaid, duly authorized to administer oaths,  Cuffee Bolze
aged  60      years, a resident of  Green Pond             County of
Colleton        and State of  South Carolina    well known
to me to be reputable and entitled to credit, and who, being duly sworn, declared in relation to
aforesaid case as follows:
         that he knows Flora Robinson well,
also knew Harry Robinson, was with him in same Company
and Regiment with them, that Flora the Widow of
Harry Robinson has never married since her
husband's death, she is old and infirm, has totally
no means of support and owns no property.


H's Post-Office address is  Green Pond, Colleton Co. S.C.
He further declare that  he has  no interest in said case and  is   not concerned
in its prosecution.

D. B. Grader                            Cuffee  his
                                               X Bolze
