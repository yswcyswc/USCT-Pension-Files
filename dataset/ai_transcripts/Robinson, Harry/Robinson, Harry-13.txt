                            GENERAL AFFIDAVIT.

State of  South Carolina, County of  Colleton                          SS:
    In the matter of  Harry Robinson late of Co G. 34th Regi-
ment U.S.C.I.
On this  23      day of  May          A. D. one thousand eight hundred and
ninety- two, personally appeared before me  C. G. Henderson a Master
in Equity                    within and for the County and State
aforesaid, duly authorized to administer oaths,  Jno Green
aged  65     years, a resident of  Green Pond             County of
Colleton        and State of  South Carolina    well known
to me to be reputable and entitled to credit, and who, being duly sworn, declared in relation to
aforesaid case as follows:
         That he knew Harry Robinson,
well, also knew Flora Robinson the widow, that she is
alive, but old and infirm, has never married
since her husband's death, that she owns no
property and has no means of support.


H's Post-Office address is  Green Pond  Colleton County S.C.
He further declare that  he has  no interest in said case and  is   not concerned
in its prosecution.
