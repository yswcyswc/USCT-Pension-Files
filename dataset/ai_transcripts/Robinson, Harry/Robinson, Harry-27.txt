        Widow's Declaration for Pension or Increase of Pension.

        This must be Executed before a Court of Record or some Officer thereof having Custody of the Seal.

State of  South Carolina , County of  Colleton                          , SS.

    ON THIS  14th  day of  October  A. D., one thousand, eight hundred and eighty 3
personally appeared before me  C. W. Fraser  of the  Circuit Court   a Court
of Record within and for the County and State aforesaid  Flora Robinson
aged  60   years, who, being duly sworn according to law, makes the following declaration in order to obtain
the Pension provided by Acts of Congress granting pension to widows: That she is the widow of  Harry
Robinson      , who enlisted under the name of  Harry Robinson
at                      on the  3rd  day of  June   A. D. 1863
in  Co G. 34th  U.S. Colored Troops,
in the war of  Rebellion   who  was discharged
                                 on account of old Age
on the _____ day of  June   A. D. 1865,
who bore at the time of his death the rank of  Private   in  his Service aforesaid
that she was married under the name of  Flora Moultrie          to said  Harry Robinson
on the _____ day of  before the war  18___ ,
by  ___J. Heyward           at  Cypress Plm  Colleton Co  there being no legal
barrier to said marriage.  Was re-married "under the flag"
at Pigeon Pt. by Chaplain Moore
That she has not remarried since the death of the said  Harry Robinson          .
