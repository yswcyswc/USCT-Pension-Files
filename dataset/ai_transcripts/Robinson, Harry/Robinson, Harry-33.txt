                            CLAIMANT'S AFFIDAVIT.

State of  South Carolina , County of  Colleton                          , SS.
    In the matter of  Flora Robinson Widow of Harry Robinson

    ON THIS  28      day of  March   A. D. 1892, personally appeared before me, a
Master in Equity          in and for the aforesaid County, duly authorized to administer oaths.
Flora Robinson          aged  61     years, a resident of  ___
in the County of  Colleton         and State of  South Carolina
whose Post Office address is  Walterboard — Colleton Co S.C.
Of C. G. Henderson
well known to me to be reputable and entitled to credit, and who, being duly sworn declared in relation to said case as
follows:
         That her Husband Harry Robinson late a Member
(Private) in Co G. 34th Regt. U.S.C.I. died at his
home in Colleton County on or about January
10th 1875, having been sick during his service and
discharged for same by Dr. Hansam Regimental
Surgeon, from which sickness he never recov-
ered, his disease was acute rheumatism from
which he died.    He was buried at the
Cypress plantation in above County —
     She has no living Children — has
had 26, but all dead.  same


James F. Mack                           Flora  her
                                              X Robinson
