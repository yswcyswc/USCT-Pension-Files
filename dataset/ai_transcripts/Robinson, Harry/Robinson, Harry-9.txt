                            GENERAL AFFIDAVIT.

State of South Carolina, County of Colleton                         , SS:
    In the matter of  Flora Robinson widow of Harry
Robinson late Private Co G. 34th Regt. U.S.C.I.
On this  18      day of  March         A. D. one thousand eight hundred and
ninety- two, personally appeared before me  C. G. Henderson Master
in Equity                    within and for the County and State
aforesaid, duly authorized to administer oaths,  Cuffee Balles
aged  60     years, a resident of  ___              County of
Colleton        and State of  South Carolina    well known
to me to be reputable and entitled to credit, and who, being duly sworn, declared in relation to
aforesaid case as follows:
That he was a private in Co G. 34 Reg. U.S.C.I.
with Harry Robinson, that he was discharged for
sickness about two months before he was presented
by Dr. Hansam Regimental Surgeon, his disease
being acute rheumatism, from which he never
recovered, and died from that disease in January
1875. Deponent being present at the time of his
death. He was then buried at the Cypress Plantation
in Colleton County. So Cypress is Harry Robinson.
Deponent now draws a pension himself.


H's Post-Office address is  Green Pond, Colleton County S. C.
He further declare that  he has  no interest in said case and  is   not concerned
in its prosecution.

James F. Mack                            Cuffee  his
                                                  X Balles
