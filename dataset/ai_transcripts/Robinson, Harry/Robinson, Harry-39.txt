                            War Department,
                        ADJUTANT GENERAL'S OFFICE,
                            Washington,  Mar 21st  , 188 3.

SIR:
    Your inquiry of  November 19th  , 188 2  in Pension Claim No. 310257,
is herewith returned, with the following information:

    Harry Robinson             was enrolled on the  1st         day of
June      , 186 3, at  Beaufort, S.C.          , as a  Private
in Co.  G  ,  34th    Regiment of U. S. Colored  Troops          , to serve  3   years.
He is reported on Muster Roll  from organization to June 3, 1863, present
for duty; July & Aug present "Sick in quar-
ters"; Sept & Oct 63 mto. Oct 3 1864, present for
duty; Nov & Dec 64 mto. Jan & Feb 65 absent,
Lft in Camp at Jacksonville, Fla.; Mar &
Apr present for duty; May & June "Discharg-
ed 4 June 2, 1865." He was discharged June
2nd 1865 at Jacksonville Fla. on Surgeons
Certificate of Disability. Certificate of Dis-
ability for discharge sent to Pension Office
January 27th 1866.


    I am, sir, very respectfully,
        Your obedient servant,

                                        M. Barber
                                             Assistant Adjutant General.
                                        By
                                             L. A. Ardmand [?]  STEREOD

The Commissioner of Pensions,
    Washington, D. C.
