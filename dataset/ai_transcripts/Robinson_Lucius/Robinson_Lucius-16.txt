DECLARATION FOR INVALID PENSION.
ACT OF JUNE, 27, 1890.

To be executed before any officer authorized to administer oaths for general purposes in the State, city, or county where said officer
resides. If such officer has a seal and uses it upon such paper, no certificate of a county clerk or prothonetary or clerk of a court shall be

clerk shall affix his official seal thereto, and shall certify to the signature and official character of said officer.

by manual labor by reason of  Rheumatism, fits and
